# Product Brief

产品简介

## Product Positioning

COC KP Helper is a Keeper-focused investigation control panel for Call of Cthulhu 7th Edition.

COC KP Helper 是一款面向《克苏鲁的呼唤》第 7 版守秘人的调查控制台。

The product is not just a note-taking tool. Its core purpose is to help a Keeper:

它不只是一个记笔记工具。它的核心目标是帮助守秘人：

- prepare interconnected stories
- run sessions with fast access to structured information
- react to unexpected player decisions
- keep investigations moving when key clues are missed

- 准备相互关联、逻辑连贯的故事
- 在跑团中快速获取结构化信息
- 响应玩家的临场突发行动
- 在关键线索遗漏时保持调查继续推进

## Core Design Principles

核心设计原则

1. Investigation first
   The app should make clue flow and scenario logic visible.
2. Run at the table
   Information should be retrievable in seconds during play.
3. COC-specific support
   Clue props, sanity pressure, secrets, horror pacing, and fallback clues should be first-class concepts.
4. Clear split between truth and player knowledge
   The Keeper needs to track both what is true and what players currently believe.

1. 调查优先
   应用应该让线索流向和剧本逻辑清晰可见。
2. 服务跑团现场
   守秘人应能在几秒内找到需要的信息。
3. 面向 COC 的专项支持
   线索道具、理智压力、秘密信息、恐怖节奏和备用线索都应是核心概念。
4. 明确区分真相与玩家认知
   守秘人需要同时记录“事实是什么”和“玩家目前相信什么”。

## Primary Users

主要用户

- New Keepers who need structure and safety rails
- Experienced Keepers who want a fast scenario dashboard
- Campaign-oriented Keepers who need continuity across sessions

- 需要结构化辅助和安全护栏的新手守秘人
- 希望拥有快速剧本指挥台的经验守秘人
- 需要跨场次连续管理内容的长期战役型守秘人

## Primary User Problems

主要用户问题

- Notes are scattered and hard to search mid-session
- Critical clues can be missed and stall the investigation
- NPC motives and relationships are difficult to keep consistent
- Clue props are hard to organize and reveal at the right time
- Session notes do not always translate into clean next-session prep

- 笔记分散，跑团中难以快速检索
- 关键线索可能被错过，导致调查停滞
- NPC 的动机和关系难以长期保持一致
- 线索道具难以整理，也不容易在合适时机展示
- 场次记录无法顺畅转化为下次开团准备

## Product Differentiation

产品差异化

Most adjacent tools focus on one of the following:

现有相近工具通常只专注于以下某一个方向：

- campaign wiki management
- VTT presentation
- generic AI assistance

- 战役 wiki / 资料管理
- VTT 呈现
- 通用 AI 助手

COC KP Helper should combine:

COC KP Helper 应该把下面几件事组合起来：

- structured investigation design
- at-table response support
- COC-flavored scenario tooling

- 结构化调查设计
- 跑团现场响应支持
- 带有 COC 风格的剧本工具

That combination is the core product edge.

这种组合能力，就是这个产品最核心的竞争力。
